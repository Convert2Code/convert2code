Never Coded Before?

If you have never coded before, we will have code mentors, introductory workshops, and code starter packs just for you!

Free. Always.

If you are worried about cost, don't be. Our core principle is the Philosophy of Free, that no matter who you are, where you come from or what you believe, you are welcome, free of charge, to any Convert to Code event, for as long as we are in operation. Period.

What is a hackathon?

A Hackathon is a 'coding marathon' where you convert your crazy ideas and passions, into code.

hack

/hak/

v. to achieve novel and clever outcomes by creatively overcoming limitations of software systems

FAQs

Who Can Come?

Anyone! If you are an experienced coder, come! If you have never seen a line of code in your life, come! If you love computers or just want to learn more about how they work, we have something for everyone!

Cost?

Free. Period.

Where and where will the Holiday Hackathon be?

Listrak has graciously allowed us to use their headquarters to host this event! Their headquarters are located at 100 W Millport Rd, Lititz, PA 17543. We will hack for six hours straight(9:30 - 3:30)

What if I have Never Coded Before?

We have code starter packs, introductory workshops, and code mentors all to get you started so, by the end of the hackathon, you can build something you are proud of!

What Should I Bring?

You should bring your computer, a charger, any snacks and of course, your imagination!
