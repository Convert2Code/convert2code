What is a hackathon?

A Hackathon is a 'coding marathon' where you convert your crazy ideas and passions into code.

Never Coded Before?

No problem! We’ll have code mentors, introductory workshops, and code starter packs just for you!

Cost?

#HolidayHack2018 is completely free of charge! No matter who you are or where you come from, you are always welcome to any Convert to Code event at no cost.

FAQs

Where and When?

Listrak, is graciously hosting #HolidayHack2018 at their headquarters on Saturday, December 15, 2018 from 9:30 AM to 3:30 PM. We will hack for 6 hours straight!

Who can come?

Everyone is welcome. If you are an experienced coder, come! If you’ve never seen a line of code in your life, come! If you love computers or just want to learn more about how they work, we have something for everyone.

What should I bring?

  • laptop (not tablet)
  • charger
  • snacks
  • your imagination!

What if I don’t know how to code?

To help you learn, we have code starter packs, introductory workshops, and code mentors on hand. By the end of the hackathon, you should be able to build something you are proud of!

Other Questions?

Contact us at converttocode@gmail.com! We’ll get back to you at our earliest convenience. Additionally, if you’d like to help us out, we’d love to hear from you! :)
