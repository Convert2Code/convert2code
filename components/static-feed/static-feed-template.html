Convert to Code empowers students to become code-literate.

We serve the greater Lancaster community by offering free coding workshops, providing access to real-life developers and real-time computer science instruction—two things many school districts within Lancaster, PA, lack. We strive to help middle school and high school students develop foundational coding skills in an increasingly tech-saturated society.

Our Inspiration

Technology is everywhere. It’s impossible to avoid it, but it is possible to demystify it. We believe learning the basics of programming is vital to understanding that computers, applications, and artificial intelligence aren’t magic: you can learn how they work. We want to give you a glimpse of the infinite possibilities learning to code has to offer.

People make other people better. Trying to learn something on your own can be scary, especially if you don’t know where to start. We want to provide you with a supportive community as you begin (or continue) your coding journey, and we hope you’ll grow alongside each other and cheer each other on. Two brains have more brainpower than one, and five brains have more brainpower than two. Learning from and with your peers and educators can be incredibly rewarding in more ways than you might think.

Everyone deserves to learn. Pursuing growth and knowledge is an admirable goal. We never want anything to stand in the way of anyone’s opportunity to learn and grow, which is why all of our events are free—your skill level, ability to pay, and access to resources don’t affect whether you can come to our workshops. We always want Convert to Code to be accessible, and that means accessible to everyone.

Our Story

Technology is everywhere. It’s impossible to avoid it, but it is possible to demystify it. We believe learning the basics of programming is vital to understanding that computers, applications, and artificial intelligence aren’t magic: you can learn how they work. We want to give you a glimpse of the infinite possibilities learning to code has to offer.

After a little deliberation, they realized one glaring issue: Lancaster didn’t have the audience for hackathons...in fact, Lancaster didn’t have much computer science curriculum at all. Copious amounts of coffee and brainstorming later, the two transformed Caleb’s initial idea into hosting free coding workshops to encourage interest in CS, equip students with programming basics, and motivate kids to learn.

Equal parts tenacity, belief in Convert to Code’s mission, and a shared love of problem-solving inspired Caleb and Danae to develop their idea, reach out to strangers, and earnestly share their vision in hopes of making it a reality. With the help of many generous individuals, Convert to Code’s first coding workshop took place on June 28, 2017, and was very well-received. A mere 15 months, 4 team members, and 13 workshops later, Convert to Code continues to grow and is excited to share what is in-store next.
